Interest

Robotics.

Machines that move, think, and act. From tiny microcontrollers to autonomous drones — the intersection of hardware and software fascinates me endlessly.

Focus Areas

What Gets Me Excited.

🚁
Drones & UAVs

Unmanned aerial vehicles are the perfect blend of electronics, mechanics, and control systems. From FPV racing drones to autonomous UAVs — the technology is endlessly fascinating.

🤖
Autonomous Bots

Robots that sense their environment and make decisions. Line-followers, obstacle avoiders, and more complex autonomous systems — building something that moves on its own is deeply satisfying.

🔌
Microcontroller Projects

Arduino, ESP32, and other microcontrollers as the brain of small hardware projects. Turning lines of code into real-world physical actions — that's where EEE gets exciting.

⚙️
Control Systems

PID controllers, feedback loops, servo motors — the engineering behind making machines behave precisely. A key part of EEE that connects directly to robotics.

📡
Wireless & IoT

Connecting robots and devices to the internet, controlling them remotely, sending sensor data wirelessly. The future of engineering is connected.

🔋
Power Electronics

Managing power for motors and systems efficiently. Battery management, motor drivers, and power circuits are the unsung heroes of every robot that moves.
